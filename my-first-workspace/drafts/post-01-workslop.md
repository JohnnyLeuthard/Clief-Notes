My favorite new word of 2026 so far? **Workslop.**

It's what happens when the CEO says "we need an AI strategy," the VP translates that into "everyone must use AI," and the rest of us start pasting ChatGPT summaries into documents nobody reads.

Tom Fishburne nailed it in his latest cartoon. An ill-defined AI strategy at the top creates poor alignment all the way down. The result isn't innovation. It's busywork with extra steps.

The thing is, AI can genuinely help. But only when people know *why* they're using it, not just that they're supposed to.

If your team's AI strategy is "just use it more," you don't have a strategy. You have a mandate to generate workslop.

The fix is simple. Start with the problem, not the tool.
